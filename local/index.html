Local information

Transportation

From the airport

From Bergen Airport the light rail called 'Bybanen' goes to the city centre. Bybanen stops at the campus on Kronstad. (The stop is called Kronstad.) A ticket with Bybanen costs 49 NOK (approximately 4 euros) and takes approximately 30 minutes to Kronstad and 45 minutes to the city centre. Bybanen is the best and cheapest alternative if you are arriving by plane. Tickets can be bought at every Bybanen-stop, and the card used to pay functions as the ticket.

There are airport buses running between the airport and the city centre. These do not stop at the campus. A ticket with the airport bus costs approximately 150 NOK.

A taxi from Bergen Airport Flesland takes approximately 30 minutes to the campus at Kronstad, depending on traffic.

Getting to campus from the city centre

If you arrive by train or boat, you will need to go to the campus from the city centre. The easiest way to get to Campus Kronstad, is to take the light rail, 'Bybanen.' The campus has a stop with the same name. Both Bybanen-lines stop at campus Kronstad.

Hotels

There are three hotels within walking distance from the campus:
Citybox Danmarksplass (1 km)
Prize by Radisson, Solheimsviken Bergen (1.2 km)
Moxy Bergen (1.4 km)

Apart from these three, there are lots of hotels in the city center, and access to the campus from the city center is easy with the Bybanen.
